Meltix Refund Policy

We handle every order with care. If a payment succeeds but your order cannot be fulfilled because of stock or verification issues, our team initiates a refund through the same payment source whenever possible.

Order Processing

Orders are confirmed only after payment verification and final stock confirmation. Custom or personalized details are reviewed as part of fulfillment.

Refund Cases

  • Failed stock confirmation after payment
  • Duplicate payment capture
  • Platform-side payment verification failure after successful charge

Support

If you need help with a payment, please contact Meltix support through the contact page and include your order code or payment reference.
